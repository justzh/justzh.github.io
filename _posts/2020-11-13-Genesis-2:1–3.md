**¹So in the heavens and the earth and everything in them were completed. ²On the seventh day God had completed his work that he had done, and he rested on the seventh day from all his work that he had done. ³God blessed the seventh day and declared it holy, for on it he rested from all his work of creation.**

By the seventh day, God had completed his work. Everything in the heavens and the earth were completed. He blessed the seventh day and declared it holy, and he rested from His work.

The seventh day in modern, human times is often known as Sunday or the Lord's Day. It is a day made for rest after working for the rest of the week. Some people use it to worship or study God's Word. This idea of the seventh day leads to the idea of the Sabbath, the holy day in which one should rest. It is contentious as to which day in modern times is the Sabbath: Saturday or Sunday? I think that the main point is that one should rest after working for six days of the week as the body, mind, and spirit need that rest to be rejuvenated and continue living a fulfilled life.
