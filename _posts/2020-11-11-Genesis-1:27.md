**²⁷So God created man in his own image; he created him in the the image of God; he created them male and female.**

After saying "Let us make man in our image, according to our likeness" in Gen. 1:26, God indeed created man in his own image: the image of God. He created them male and female. Even though we are human beings, we are still made in the image of God. There are two sexes: male and female.

The annotations in CSB refer to Gn 5:2, Mt 19:4, and Mk 10:6.
