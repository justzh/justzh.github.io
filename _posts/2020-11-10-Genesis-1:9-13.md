**<sup>9</sup>Then God said, "Let the water under the sky be gathered into one place, and let the dry land appear." And it was so. <sup>10</sup>God called the dry land "earth," and the gathering of the water he called "seas." And God saw that it was good. <sup>11</sup>Then God said, "Let the earth produce vegetation: seed-bearing plants and fruit trees on the earth bearing fruit with seed in it according to their kinds." And it was so. <sup>12</sup>The earth produced vegetation: seed-bearing plants according to the kinds and trees bearing fruit with seed in it according to their kinds and trees bearing fruit with seed in it according to their kinds. And God saw that it was good. <sup>13</sup>Evening came and then morning: the third day.**
