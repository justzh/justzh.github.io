**<sup>24</sup>Then God said, "Let the earth produce living creatures according to their kinds: livestock, creatures that crawl, and the wildlife of the earth according to their kinds." And it was so. <sup>25</sup>So God made the wildlife of the earth according to their kinds, and all the creatures that crawl on the ground according to their kinds. And God saw that it was good.**

Seventh time God speaks. He creates livestock, creatures that crawl, and the wildlife of the earth.

He is creating land animals here.

From what I know, livestock includes cattle, sheep, goats, pigs, and chickens. Creatures that crawl can include insects and lizards. Wildlife is every other land animal not forementioned.

GOD saw it was good.
