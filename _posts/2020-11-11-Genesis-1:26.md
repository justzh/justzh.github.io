**<sup>26</sup>Then God said, "Let us make man in our image, according to our likeness. They will rule the fish of the sea, the birds of the sky, the livestock, the whole earth, and the creatures that crawl on the earth."**

God speaks for the eighth time.

Man is made is God's image! We are an image of God!!

He gives us rule over the fish, the birds, the livestock, the WHOLE earth, and the creatures that crawl on the earth. Not wildlife though or some of the swarming creatures in the sea.
